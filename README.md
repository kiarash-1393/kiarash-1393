### ⚽ Real-Time Football Intelligence Engine

*Turning raw match footage into professional-grade tactical intelligence — the same category of system used by elite football analytics departments.*

</div>

**Tactical Vision AI** is a full computer-vision pipeline that converts unedited match footage into structured, actionable tactical data in real time. It fuses **object detection, multi-object tracking, geometric projection, and statistical modeling** into a single cohesive Streamlit-powered analytics dashboard.

<table>
<tr>
<th>🧩 Module</th>
<th>⚙️ Capability</th>
<th>📌 Status</th>
</tr>
<tr><td><b>Player Detection</b></td><td>YOLO-based real-time multi-class detection across all players</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Ball Tracking</b></td><td>High-speed ball trajectory tracking with occlusion recovery</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Team Classification</b></td><td>Automatic jersey-color clustering to separate teams & GK</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Heatmaps</b></td><td>Positional density mapping per player & per team</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Pass Detection</b></td><td>Event-based pass recognition with directionality</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>xG Analysis</b></td><td>Calibrated expected-goals model (Logistic Regression / XGBoost)</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Pressing Analysis</b></td><td>Defensive pressure intensity & PPDA-style metrics</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Formation Detection</b></td><td>Dynamic tactical shape inference from player positions</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Real-Time Analytics</b></td><td>Live-updating stats layer synced to video playback</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Streamlit Dashboard</b></td><td>Interactive control center for coaches & analysts</td><td>✅ Production</td></tr>
</table>
